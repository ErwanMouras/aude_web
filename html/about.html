About

Founded in 2008 in Copenhagen, Norm Architects is a multidisciplinary architecture and design practice creating spaces and objects that enrich the human experience.

Our Philosophy

We believe in creating designs that "enrich the human experience" through minimalism with a goal of finding design "essence". Our approach seeks to connect designs with "innate human desire for belonging, autonomy and identity".

Every project is approached with a holistic mindset, considering the relationship between form, function, and the human experience. We aim to "re-sensualize the built environment" through thoughtful design that emphasizes natural materials, light, and spatial quality.

Studio History

Founded in 2008 in Copenhagen, Norm Architects has grown into an internationally recognized practice with 15 years of experience spanning residential, hospitality, and industrial design projects worldwide.

Our multidisciplinary approach allows us to work across scales and typologies, from large architectural projects to intimate product designs, always maintaining our commitment to soft minimalism and human-centered design principles.
