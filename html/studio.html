Our Studio

A multidisciplinary practice founded in Copenhagen, creating architecture and design that enhances the human experience through thoughtful minimalism.

Design Approach

Our work spans residential, hospitality, and commercial architecture, as well as furniture, lighting, and product design. Each project is approached with the same fundamental principles: respect for materials, consideration of human scale, and a deep understanding of how people inhabit space.

We believe that good design should be both functional and beautiful, creating environments that support well-being while expressing a clear aesthetic vision rooted in Scandinavian design traditions.

Recognition

Our work has been featured in leading design publications including Wallpaper*, Dezeen, Architectural Digest, and Kinfolk. We have received numerous awards for our architectural and design projects, and our work is part of permanent collections at design museums worldwide.
